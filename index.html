    • P2P: PEER TO PEER

    Peer-to-peer is the exchange or sharing of information, data or assets between parties without the involvement of a central authority. Peer-to-peer, or P2P, takes a decentralized approach to interactions between individuals and groups. This approach has been used in computers and networks (peer-to-peer file sharing), as well as with currency trading (virtual currencies).
